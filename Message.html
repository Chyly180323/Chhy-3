Welcome to All Students
First of all, congratulations to those who have passed the High School Diploma examination during the reform era of the Ministry of Education, Youth and Sport. However, we also encourage those who did not pass not to lose hope in their studies, as you can still enroll in higher education institutions in all majors just like those who passed, by studying for an Associate Degree and then transferring credits to pursue a Bachelor's Degree. We would like to inform all of you that Western University is on the right path in the education sector by prioritizing teaching and learning quality, curriculum, discipline, and services as core strategies for training. Western University's curriculum also integrates both Hard Skills and Soft Skills for every student. As a result, in recent years, the number of students enrolling at Western University has continued to increase steadily, even though the number of students passing the Baccalaureate exam has decreased following the reforms by the Ministry of Education, Youth and Sport.
